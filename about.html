About Me

Whether we’re sharing cocktails over candlelight, enjoying a refined dinner, or escaping together for a private getaway, I pride myself on being attentive, playful, and genuinely present. How to Book

All booking inquiries are submitted through the booking form on this site. Please include your preferred date, location, and any relevant details so your request may be reviewed with care.

Screening & Verification
For the comfort, discretion, and peace of mind of both parties, screening and verification are required for all guests. Requests that cannot be verified may not be considered.

Availability & Notice
Advance notice is always preferred. However, a limited number of established or pre-approved guests may be accommodated with two hours or less notice, subject to availability and successful verification.

Confirmation
Once your request is accepted, you will receive a confirmation message with next steps and deposit details to reserve our time together.

Your privacy is respected at every stage. All communication is handled discreetly, professionally, and with intention.
